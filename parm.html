It's one of those everyone-should-know-how-to-make recipes. It's easy and comes together quickly. In fact, it's hard to mess up! Chicken Parmesan is also one of the best uses I can think of for boneless, skinless, chicken breasts.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Chicken cutlets
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil

Follow these easy steps:

  1. Buy Ingredients
  2. Dredge and cook chicken cutlets
  3. Cook noodles
  4. Assemble in layers
  5. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees
  6. Top with sprigs of fresh basil
  7. Let rest for 5-10 minutes and serve with red wine
